About Me

Since youth, I've always enjoyed two things - telling stories and helping others. In elementary and middle school, I read book after book at the library and wrote weird stories in my notebook. In high school, I volunteered at soup kitchens and tutored fellow students. In college, I earned degrees in English and Education, and taught classes as a student teacher. But I began to explore other career paths during the pandemic. I found myself drawn to technical writing. Helping others by telling the stories of products in a digestible way felt like the perfect marriage of the two core parts of my identity. So here I am - I hope we can work together!

My non-reading and writing hobbies include playing piano, swimming, climbing, sketching, solving puzzles, working out, and learning programming.
